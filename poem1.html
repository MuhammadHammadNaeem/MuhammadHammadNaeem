Feathers flying in the night,
Children gather, faces bright.
Pillows soft, in hands held tight,
It's a wondrous pillow fight.

Swinging softly, laughter's song,
Pillow warriors all night long.
Giggles echo, spirits light,
In this cozy, feathery delight.

Duck and dodge with all your might,
Pillow clouds in soft moonlight.
Bedtime whispers, eyes shut tight,
Dreams take flight on pillows light.
